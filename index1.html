Disability Awareness

+

Blind

Blindness is the condition of lacking visual perception due to physiological or neurological factors.

+

Deaf

Deafness is the partial or total inability to hear sounds, which can affect communication and daily activities.

+

Dumb

The term "dumb" refers to individuals who cannot speak, often due to a physical or neurological condition.

+

Low Vision

Low vision refers to visual impairment that cannot be corrected by standard glasses or surgery.

+

Naked Prosthetics

Naked Prosthetics offers advanced prosthetics for individuals missing fingers, enhancing function and aesthetics.

+

Locometer Disability

Locometer disability affects an individual's ability to navigate and orient themselves in their environment.

+

Mental Illness

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being, affecting how we think and act.

+

Intellectual Disbility

Intellectual disability is characterized by limitations in intellectual functioning and adaptive behavior.

+

Acid Attack Victims

Intellectual disability is characterized by limitations in intellectual functioning and adaptive behavior.

+

Dyslexia

Intellectual disability is characterized by limitations in intellectual functioning and adaptive behavior.
